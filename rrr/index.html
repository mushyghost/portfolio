Game - Ron The Rabbit Run After Rooster

This game is an endless type of game. This game was originally created by Infouredges Corporation before becoming a part of Mushy Ghost.

Story

A rabbit named Ron runs after a red rooster. The reason Ron does so is the red rooster is mocking at him. Meanwhile, the rabbit is running after the red rooster and gains carrots and crystals in order to collect new suits. Because he basically likes to be glamour.

Mechanism

In this game the player must keep running through the zig-zag road. And also, the player needs to destroy every single obstacle that may appear on the road. If not, the player’s health will decrease, and when the health run out, the player will die.

In the game, there are two types of currency, that are carrots and crystals. And there are two modes in the game, that are Double Carrots Mode and Crystal Mode. In Double Carrot Mode, one carrot that can be collected will be considered as 2 carrots in a limited amount of time. This mode can be gained by collecting Double Carrot Mode item in the game. For Crystal Mode, every item in the game will change into crystals, for example, a carrot will be replaced by one crystal. This mode can be gained by collecting multiple of 3 crystals in normal mode. By using these currencies, the player can buy new clothes and increase game modes, such as more health, more time for Double Carrot Modes, and more time for Crystal Modes.

There are also challenges to complete in the game, such as run over 500 meters. Rewards after completing challenges are carrots or crystals, based on difficulty in order to complete the challenge. These rewards will be added to player currencies.

Scoring systems in the game are the distance of how far the player runs, the number of carrots, and the number of crystals. The distance will be considered as separate top score. And for the second top scoring system is the number of carrots plus the doubled number of crystals, because of crystals are rare and harder to collect. In conclusion, there are two top-scoring systems that are distance and collecting currencies.
